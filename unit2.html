Unit 2: Advanced Data Structures

Overview of Topics

This unit dives into advanced data structures like AVL Trees, Binary Heaps, and more.

AVL Trees are height-balanced binary search trees that ensure O(log n) time complexity for insertion, deletion, and search.

Red-Black Trees balance themselves through color rules, providing efficient insertion and deletion operations.

Binary Heaps support fast retrieval of the maximum or minimum element and are often used in priority queues.
